Company Profile

Support Education (SupEd) is established in 2016 based in addis Ababa Ethiopia. we are a pioneer in the localizing of digital content based on the existing curriculum of the country's educational system.
Through our in-house software development team, we continue to produce and deliver many applications that benefit high schools, preparatory schools, universities, and the entire educational community. 

For More..

Participation
Support Education’s vision is to become a “one stop education solution” in delivering quality digital educational content supported by technology related products, such as, tablets, school networking solutions and power options such as solar energy for all system hardware in schools and institutions whether or not they are on the national grid. Thus, making our projects and products appropriate for use everywhere in the country.

Our Motivation
Our motivation and driving force stems from our passion for producing localized digital content that meets the so evident national lack of textbooks and reference materials for students as well as teachers. We strive to address the impoverishment of education arising from the general lack of Internet access in most schools in the country.

Our Experience
For more than ten years, we have been installing an innovative system called eGranary and training schools and tertiary institutions on how to use this offline digital library developed and distributed by the WiderNet project affiliated with the UNC (University of North Carolina) at Chapel Hill. We availed eGranary, a solution that contains more than 32 million
educational resources to different educational and health institutions throughout Ethiopia. 

What We Do

We have deliver solutions to Ethiopian higher education, Preparatory school, high schools, and lower schools. Working with professionals from the field of ICT (Software and mobile application development, computer networking) education and management, we are offering an offline digital library system developed according to the national curriculum and following a non-structured education approach. This development includes availing digitized educational resources for all school levels of students as well as training of users.

Mission

To bring Equitable and inclusive education to all through the development and introduction of educational technology, training, and availing educational resource through offline and online means.

Vision

To build innovative and good citizenship values with in the community, to see a developed country with access to equitable and quality of education, health facilities, conducive leaving environment, resources and job opportunities, irrespective of their geographical location, ethnicity and economic status.
