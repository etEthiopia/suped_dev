Technical Group from MOE and Regional Bureau
04-12-2019

A team visited the two pilot schools, Burayu and Chacha General Secondary and Preparatory Schools,

on 14th and 15th of November 2017, to get the actual overview of the pilot project. They have discussed with the school principals, teachers and students, after having visited activities performed(Educational service, which includes educational tablet and offline digital library) with in the school compound.

“We have sent out technical team to visit the field, and the feedback proves its importance. We are convinced and agree in its implementation in the schools in the region. Supporting education through technology is one of the tools for the improvement of quality of education. I want to pass my appreciation to Support education, the local partner and UAB Sparkman center on delivering this project. ” (Mr. Tefera Bayissa, Amhara Education Bureau, Deputy Head )
